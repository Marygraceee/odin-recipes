How to make pizza!

Pizza is an italian specialty famous all around the globe.
You probably know pizza in a specific way, because every place has its own style of pizza.
The key ingredients for a good pizza are:

  • love
  • local ingredients
  • patience

Today we will learn how to make the italian variety of pizza, the Neapolitan Pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Flour
  • 120g Water
  • 50g Tomato sauce
  • 100g Mozzarella
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 10g of dried yeast

How to make Neapolitan Pizza:

  1. Combine the flour, the water and the yeast, then knead.
  2. When the dough is almost silky, add the salt, then cover the dough under a damp cloth
  3. Wait 30 minutes, then knead the dough again. After that, let the dough rest and rise for 3-4 hours
  4. When the dough is ready, place it on the table and start pressing on it, making sure not to press on the cruts, otherwise the air will escape
  5. When your dough "pie" is round and thin, spread some tomato sauce, making sure not to put it on the crust
  6. Add mozzarella, making sure not to overdo it: 100g are enough (I'm talking to you, Americans!)
  7. Put the pizza in the over at max temperature for 15 minutes. For the first 10 minutes the pizza has to cook on the lowest possible position in the oven
    In the remaining 5 minutes, the pizza has to cook on the highest spot in the oven
  8. When the pizza is cooked, place it on a plate and add extra virgin olive oil to taste

There you go! You have succesfully made a Neapolitan style pizza! People from Italy will be proud of you.
