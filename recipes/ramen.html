How to make ramen!

Ramen is a flavourful japanese dish. It's perfect for winter evenings and it gives its 100% when eaten steamingly hot.
The ingredients vary a lot based on taste: some people like it with meat, eggs, onion; some people only like the noodles and the broth.
You are totally free to choose the type of ramen you wanna eat.

Today, we're gonna explain you how to make a specific type of ramen: the "Veggie" Ramen.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles (store bought or homemade
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Zucchini
  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

How to make the Veggie Ramen:

  1. Slice the zucchini, carrots and onions in very small pieces. Cut the broccoli so you only have the florets
  2. Grab a pan and pour some sesame oil
  3. When very hot, put the vegetables into the pan and add salt
  4. Let the vegetables lightly cook, then turn off the fire
  5. Grab a pot and fill it with water. When boiling hot, put the noodles in
  6. When the noddles are almost ready, drain them and turn back on the fire under the pan
  7. When the vegetables start sizzling again, put the pasta in and a little bit of soy sauce
  8. Let fry for a bit, then turn the fire off and serve

Congratulations, you have succesfully made a Veggie Ramen!
